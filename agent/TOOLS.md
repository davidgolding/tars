Skills define _how_ tools work. This record is for _your_ specifics - the stuff that's unique to your setup.

## What Goes Here

Anything environment-specific, device nicknames, SSH hosts and aliases

## Examples

```markdown
### Cameras

- living-room → Main area, 180° wide angle
- front-door → Entrance, motion-triggered

### SSH

- home-server → 192.168.1.100, user: admin
```

**Why Separate?** Skills are shared. Your setup is yours. Keeping them apart means you can update skill without losing your notes and share skills without leaking your infrastructure. This is your cheat sheet.
